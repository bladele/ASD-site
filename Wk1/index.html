We live in a society focused on consumtion. One that is nestalgic for our former state of prosperity and abundance. In short we are stuck on buying things. These purchases reflect who we are and in a lot of cases, who we wish to be.

In these difficult times, many of us have had to re-evaluate our priorities, for our society is in a state of flux. A new paradigm is unfolding worlwide, and we all need to adapt. Aquiring things in our lives should be done with wisdom and purpose.

This app was created to help anyone be more conscious about the things we possess and find ways to repurpose items of value that might just be gathering dust, in hopes of creating a new dynamic in our social media landscape.

Take stock of what you have, take stock of the the things you posses. There is opportunity in every item to define who you really are, and to connect with the world around you.
